What is Karma.gg?

All to often we blame loses on our teamates poor performance in our games. It feels like no matter the game, the enemy summoners simply outclasses your own. This is a frustration that is felt at all ranks and by most all players. Karma.gg allows you to look back on your collective past teamates and enemies to see how they have been faring without you on their squad. By searching your name above we'll crunch the numbers and determine whether your teamates have truely been holding you back or if they have left you in the dust
